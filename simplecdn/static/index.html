HTTP/3 + QUIC Demo

Simple CDN with caching, range forwarding, and HLS delivery.

This page is served by the origin. The CDN in front of it fetches over HTTP/3, caches normal GET requests, forwards range requests directly to the origin, and can deliver the sample HLS stream under /hls/stream.m3u8.

What To Verify

  • Request the same page twice through the CDN and inspect the X-Cache header: first MISS, then HIT.
  • Fetch an HLS segment with a Range header and confirm it stays a cache MISS.
  • Watch the QUIC migration demo separately while changing the client IP and observe that the same connection survives.
